Privacy Policy

Who we are
The SEND Reform Navigator is operated by Neurodiversity Global Ltd.

What data we collect
- Analytics (with consent): Anonymous page views.
- Contact form: Name, email, message.
- Feedback and questions: Name (optional) and text.
- Child profile data: Stored temporarily (up to 14 days) then deleted. Encrypted.
- Authentication: Email and hashed password.

Lawful basis
Legitimate interest and consent.

How we use your data
- To display feedback (moderated).
- To respond to messages.
- To improve the site.
- To generate child profile documents (AI processed using the SEND Navigator knowledge base, not trained on).

Data retention
- Child profile data: Deleted after 14 days.
- Analytics: Up to 12 months.
- Contact/Feedback: Retained while site is active.

Data sharing
We do not sell data. We do not share with advertisers. AI processing via Anthropic (Claude) uses the SEND Navigator knowledge base and does not use data for training.

Cookies
Essential cookies and optional analytics cookies.

Your rights
Right to access, correct, delete, or object.
