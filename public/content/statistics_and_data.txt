Statistics and data

Key SEND statistics for England as of 07 February 2026.

Summary facts
- SEND demand continues to rise (1.7m+ pupils).
- EHCPs increased by over 10% in one year.
- Fewer than half of new EHCPs completed within 20 weeks.
- Tribunal activity remains high (25,000 appeals).
- SEND spending increased, but funding gaps remain.

Confirmed position
- 638,700 Active EHC plans (Jan 2025)
- 10.8% Annual increase
- 46% Issued within 20 weeks (2024)
- 25,000 Appeals registered (2024/25)

Funding
- High needs funding: £9.4bn (2024/25) vs £5.3bn (2014/15)
- Local authorities spent £950m additional on top of allocation.

Projected figures
- Annual DSG deficit projected to exceed £6bn in 2028/29.
