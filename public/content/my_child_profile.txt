My Child: This is me

Build a document about your child to share with professionals.

This tool helps you create a structured, professional-looking profile of your child to share with schools, SENCOs, and health professionals. It focuses on your child's strengths, needs, and what actually helps them, moving the conversation away from just "behaviour" or "deficits".
