Why I Built the SEND Navigator

I built the SEND Navigator because too many parents are fighting battles they should never have had to fight.

The reality for families
For most families, it starts small. Then the waiting begins. What should be support quickly turns into years of advocacy.

That experience changes you
It changes how you see education and power. Parents end up doing jobs they never trained for.

Why the SEND Navigator exists
It exists because parents should not have to become legal experts just to protect their children. It exists because what families are dealing with is systemic failure.

Built to change that
The SEND Navigator was built to help parents understand what is happening, what should be happening, and where they stand in the process. It is about clarity instead of confusion. Power through understanding, not conflict.
