How to use this guide

Two navigation bars
- Top bar: Site links (About, Sources, Q&A)
- Journey tabs: Colour-coded tabs taking you through the reform story step by step.

What the colours mean
- Teal: Confirmed (Official facts)
- Amber: Under discussion (Proposals)
- Orange: Unconfirmed / Leaked (Rumours)
- Rose: What's next (Timeline)

Page structure
1. 60-second summary
2. Reading depth control
3. Information layers

Helpful features
- Dark & light mode
- Ask a question
- Reassurance first (Leading with what hasn't changed)
