Sources and Evidence Base

This page lists the sources this site draws on. It exists so you can check anything we say. Every page uses a confidence label (Confirmed, Discussed, Unconfirmed) based on these sources.

Source Categories:
- Government & Legislation (Children and Families Act 2014, SEND Code of Practice, DfE guidance)
- Official Statistics (DfE datasets, National Audit Office)
- Legal & Rights (IPSEA, Equality Act 2010)
- Clinical & Health (NHS England, NICE guidelines)
- Sector Reports (Local Government Association, National Network of Parent Carer Forums)
- Media & Leaks (Guardian, Schools Week - clearly labelled as such)

How we use sources
Every claim presented as fact traces back to a source. Unconfirmed information is labelled.
