About Neurodiversity Global

The organisation behind SEND Reform Navigator.

Neurodiversity Global is a UK based, neurodivergent led consultancy that helps organisations redesign how work, leadership, and systems operate so neurodivergent people can succeed.

We work with employers, education providers, and public bodies to move beyond awareness and into practical change.

What we deliver
- Neurodiversity and neuroinclusion workshops
- Leadership development
- Strategic advisory support
- Coaching
- System reviews
- Evidence informed resources

How we work
- Practical and evidence informed
- Designed for complex environments
- Focused on changing daily decisions
- Led by people with lived experience
