About this resource

Why this resource exists

SEND Reform Navigator exists to help people make sense of SEND reform in England. It brings information together in a clear, calm way, without adding interpretation or pressure. It explains what is known, what is being discussed, and what is not yet decided.

Who this resource is for

Parents, carers, teachers, school leaders, and professionals.

Independence and neutrality

SEND Reform Navigator is independent. It does not represent government, local authorities, schools, or advocacy groups. The content is written to be neutral and factual.

How information is updated

Information is reviewed and updated as new, confirmed details are published. Errors are corrected.

Limits of this resource

This resource does not provide legal advice. It focuses only on England. It does not replace professional support.

About the author

Rich Ferriman is the creator and editorial lead. He is an award-winning neuroinclusion consultant, founder of Neurodiversity Global, and father of four neurodivergent children.

Governance and accountability

SEND Reform Navigator is committed to transparency, independence, and clarity.
